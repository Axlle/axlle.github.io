Why “Axlle”?

Axle is a Lego minifigure from the Rock Raiders series. Around the same time that Rock Raiders was popular, one of my favourite games, RuneScape, was launched. When I was creating my RuneScape account, the name “Axle” was either taken or too short (I don’t know which) so I went with “Axlle” with two L’s.
